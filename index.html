FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything you need to know before getting started. Don't see your question here? Just ask.

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects take 2–6 weeks from kickoff to launch, depending on scope and how quickly we receive feedback and content. Larger e-commerce or custom-feature builds may take a bit longer.

How much does a website cost?

Every project is scoped individually based on the number of pages, features, and complexity involved. After a free discovery call, you'll get a clear, fixed-price quote — no surprise fees.

Do I own the website once it's finished?

Yes. Once the project is paid in full, the code, design files, and content are yours outright — no lock-in and no recurring licensing fees.

What do you need from me to get started?

A short brief about your business and goals, any branding assets you already have (logo, colors, fonts), and content for each page — or we can help you write it.

Do you offer support after launch?

Yes. We offer optional maintenance and support plans for updates, hosting, and security patches — or you can manage everything yourself with a simple, easy-to-use CMS.

Can you redesign or improve my existing website?

Absolutely. We regularly take over existing sites to modernize the design, improve performance, and fix usability issues — without losing your current SEO rankings.
